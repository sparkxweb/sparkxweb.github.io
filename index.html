About

SparkX is local project started by 2 high schoolers, Anshul Shah and Rohan Nalla, in an effort to help local businesses and organizations modernize their E-commerce. Mainly focusing on businesses who were hit hard by Covid-19 and/or do not have an adequate budget for website devlopment, SparkX works with these organizations to deliver a high quality website at little to no charge. In addition to this, SparkX also provides other amenities such as SEO optimization and logo/branding services.
